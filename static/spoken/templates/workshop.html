About

This workshop introduces two Free and Open Source Software for Chemistry education, namely, GChemPaint and Jmol Application. These software are useful for students, educators and researchers of Chemistry and Bio-Chemistry.

Workshop is aimed to give Chemistry teachers a practical experience in using these software. Teachers will learn and practice the software using Spoken Tutorials of GChemPaint and Jmol, followed by a hands-on session. Workshop also includes an Interactive Session for feedback and improvement of the tutorial content.

Note: All the participants should bring their laptop with chargers and headphones/earphones for the workshop.

Workshop Objectives

   To promote the usage of technology in classroom teaching.

   To raise the standard of classroom teaching using Software.

   Software helps teachers explain a concept with more clarity.

   Better presentation of topics.
