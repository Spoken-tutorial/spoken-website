Induction Programme of PMMMNMTT
Expression of Interest

Dear Colleague,
I am happy to invite your Expression of Interest to the proposed induction programme for college teachers, to be organised at IIT Bombay, under the aegis of the Teaching Learning Centre (ICT), funded by the Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya National Mission on Teachers and Teaching, an initiative of MHRD. All details about this course are available at http://spoken-tutorial.org/induction/. Your inputs to this survey will be useful for the following: (1) shortlisting candidates, in case there are many applicants (2) deciding the number of rooms to be provided in boys or girls hostels (3) deciding the experts to invite and examples to include, depending on the background of participants. We shall also use this for research purposes, so as to improve such training programmes.
